biography

Furthering my passion for software development and penchant for bleeding-edge technologies, I provide an array of digital services to businesses large and small.

I have spent my entire life creating systems and programs. I consider well-organized, intricate yet complex structures a thing of pure beauty. "Work smart" is not solely a professional philosophy— this mantra really embodies my approach to life— I'm still a firm believer in due diligence, without strategy and focus long as my efforts are strategic and provide tangible value to my clients and community. So it fits then, that over the years I developed this passion for problem solving...addressing issues of inefficiency, resource waste, and corporate stagnation, often designing/building custom-tailored products and business solutions on the fly. Having taught myself web development, I have grown very comfortable with technical concepts of varying complexities. A true California girl through and through, I spend most of my free time at the beach; practicing yoga, or indulging in delectable vegan cuisine via the prolific vegan-friendly Angeleno restaurants...and creating my own versions at home in my kitchen!
